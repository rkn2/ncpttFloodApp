A one-off event such as a flood should not cause serious damage as long as the wood is allowed to dry afterwards. Problems occur when moisture is trapped in the wood and cannot escape. This can happen in wall cavities, in sill plates under floors, behind panelling and under impermeable finishes such as oil-based gloss paint.
