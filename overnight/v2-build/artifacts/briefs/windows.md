[49] These photos from an industrial mill in Baltimore, MD, show (a) the structural reinforcing required at large window openings that extend below the established flood risk level. Rather than blocking these windows, either permanently or temporarily, the owner elected to use aquarium glass in the lower sash. The steel reinforcing will later be concealed by wood trim. Certain compromises in the glass color and transparency, as well as frame details and muntin profiles, were necessary to accommodate the thicker glass in the lower sash.
