## Notable gaps (nothing publicly available)
- **Insurance guides specific to historic properties** — no insurer (State Farm, Chubb, Nationwide) publishes a downloadable historic-property flood recovery guide; National Trust Insurance Services exists but no public PDF
- **SBA historic-specific guidance** — SBA doesn't publish separate guidance for historic property owners; they use the standard physical disaster loan materials
- **University extension publications specific to historic/pre-1940s construction** — extension guides are all for modern homes
