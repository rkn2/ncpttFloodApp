What do the damage levels mean?

None No visible damage to this part of the building. It looks and works normally.
Minor Cosmetic damage only — staining, peeling paint, surface dirt. The structure and function are still intact.
Moderate Damage affects how the building looks or works, but it's not about to collapse. Professional inspection is recommended.
Severe Major loss of material, structural compromise, or immediate safety concern. Do not use this part of the building until a professional has assessed it.
